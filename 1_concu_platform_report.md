# Full Platform Report: Concu-Inspired Delivery System

## Overview
This report outlines the information required to build a premium, delivery-only bakery/patisserie platform inspired by Concu. The focus is on selling pastries, regular cakes, and custom cakes with a dedicated in-house delivery fleet.

## 1. Information to Extract from the Client
To begin development, please gather the following details from your client:

### A. Branding & Aesthetics
*   **Logo & Brand Colors:** Exact color codes (HEX/RGB) and high-resolution logos.
*   **Typography:** Preferred fonts (e.g., elegant, modern serif or clean sans-serif like Concu).
*   **Imagery:** High-quality photos of pastries, cakes, and the cafe. (Premium imagery is crucial for this aesthetic).

### B. Product & Menu Details
*   **Menu Categories:** e.g., Signature Cakes, Custom Cakes, Pastries, Breads, Beverages.
*   **Custom Cake Requirements:** What exact customization options do they offer? (e.g., Weight, Shape, Flavor, Sponge type, Frosting, Custom text/messages, Image uploads).
*   **Pricing Structure:** Base prices, tax percentages (GST/VAT), and delivery fee logic (flat rate or distance-based).

### C. Operational Logistics
*   **Delivery Radii:** What is the maximum distance they will deliver to?
*   **Working Hours:** Order acceptance hours (e.g., 10 AM to 10 PM) and lead time for custom cakes (e.g., 24-48 hours).
*   **Delivery Boys:** How many delivery personnel do they have? Do they need a separate mobile view/app for delivery boys to accept and track orders?

### D. Order Verification
*   **Phone Verification:** Since they strictly verify via phone, what is the exact script/process? Should the system automatically trigger an SMS saying "We will call you shortly to confirm your order"?

### E. Legal & Policies
*   **Terms of Service & Privacy Policy:** Handling customer data (Name, Phone, Address).
*   **Refund/Cancellation Policy:** Crucial for perishable goods like cakes.
